How to Play

Objective: Avoid eating the spoiled sweet (bottom-left cell)!

How to Play:
Players take turns choosing a mithai. On selecting any mithai, all sweets to the right and above it get removed. The player forced to take the spoiled sweet loses.

Example: Click on a mithai at Row 2, Col 3 — all sweets in its row and above it (to the right) disappear.

Note: The spoiled sweet is at the bottom-left corner (D1). Avoid it!
